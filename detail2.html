Elementary School

During my elementary school years, my motivation to learn came from curiosity. I was eager to understand new things and enjoyed discovering basic knowledge. This stage helped me develop an early interest in learning and self-improvement.

Junior High School

In junior high school, my motivation grew as I began to understand the importance of education. I became more aware of my responsibilities as a student and was motivated to perform better academically and personally.

Senior High School

At the senior high school level, my motivation became more goal-oriented. I started thinking seriously about my future and the direction I wanted to take after graduation. This phase encouraged me to work harder, improve my skills, and prepare myself for higher education.

University

At university, my motivation is driven by self-development and future career goals. As an Information Systems student, I am motivated to learn how technology and information systems can be applied to solve real-world problems. I am committed to continuous learning, improving my skills, and becoming a professional who can contribute positively to society.
